⚡ Strava auto-sync

Once set up, this app checks Strava every time you open it — no button, no manual step. New runs get matched to their plan day automatically.
Runs are matched to plan days by date; multiple runs on the same day are summed. Auto-sync fills empty days with your Strava actuals and updates previously-synced days on re-open — it never overwrites a day you've logged by hand. Setup steps are in the project README.
